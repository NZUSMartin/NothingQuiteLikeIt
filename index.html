A beachfront estate unlike any other

Conceived for those who demand the extraordinary, Nothing Quite Like It is a new waterfront estate on the celebrated north shore of Grand Cayman — where the Caribbean meets Rum Point's legendary sunsets and the clearest water in the hemisphere.

Six en-suite bedrooms, approximately 7,500 square feet (700 sqm) of considered living space, and a private beach that belongs entirely to you. Floor-to-ceiling glass dissolves the boundary between indoors and ocean. A heated infinity pool cascades toward the sea. Every room, every detail, composed for the rare family that settles for nothing less.

Location

Rum Point, Grand Cayman

Rum Point is the quieter, more considered side of Grand Cayman — celebrated for its gin-clear water, powdery sand and the famous sunsets that paint the sky each evening. The villa sits directly on the beach at 134 Sand Pointe Road, facing west.

It is far enough from Seven Mile Beach to feel secluded, and close enough to everything that matters to remain effortlessly connected.

Rum Point Club & beach bar — minutes on foot
Kaibo Restaurant — nearby by water or road
Crystal Caves — a short drive
Stingray City — approx. 20 minutes by boat
George Town & Seven Mile Beach — approx. 1 hour
Owen Roberts International Airport — approx. 1 hour
