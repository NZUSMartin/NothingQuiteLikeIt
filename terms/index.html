4. Deposit & Balance

A non-refundable deposit of 10% of the total rental value is payable upon booking confirmation to secure the dates. The remaining balance is due no later than 90 days prior to the arrival date. For bookings made within 90 days of arrival, the full rental amount is payable at the time of booking.

5. Cancellation Policy

All deposits are non-refundable. For cancellations received:

  • More than 90 days before arrival: The deposit is forfeited. No further charge applies.

  • Less than 60 days before arrival: 100% of the total rental value is forfeited.

We strongly recommend that Guests obtain comprehensive travel insurance covering cancellation for all foreseeable reasons.
